## How to plan a Pondicherry day (the rules the planner uses)

1. **Group stops by area and route by driving time.** The planner sequences a day by nearest-neighbour driving time and groups stops by area, so you backtrack as little as possible. Each day is a **loop from your base** — you start and end at the same place.
2. **Plan a full day (~9 AM–10 PM) with meals woven in by time of day** — breakfast → lunch → snack → dinner, each at the right hour.
3. **Do White Town in the cool morning, on foot.** The French Quarter's cafés, the Ashram, churches and Bharathi Park are a few hundred metres apart — walk, don't drive. Visit the **Sri Aurobindo Ashram before its midday close**.
4. **Going to Paradise Beach? Drive south to the Chunnambar Boat House early** — it's the only access, by backwater ferry, and the **last boat is ~2:30 PM**. Pair it with a sit-down lunch at Jallikattu, opposite the jetty.
5. **Auroville / Matrimandir: collect your pass at the Auroville Visitor Centre first**, then view the golden dome. The free Viewing-Point pass is collected in person; the **inner-chamber meditation needs an advance online pass** (booked a few days ahead).
6. **Temples in the evening:** the Manakula Vinayagar Temple **reopens at 4 PM** — plan it for late afternoon.
7. **Surf lessons (Serenity Beach) in the morning** while you're fresh and the sea is calmer.
8. **Save beaches and the Promenade for golden hour** — sunset on Promenade/Rock Beach is the signature moment.
9. **Close the evening with dinner** — and craft beer / nightlife for a friends' trip.
10. **Check the day's weather.** Pondicherry's SW-monsoon afternoons bring showers and it gets hot — carry an umbrella (it doubles as shade), and the planner flags this per date.

## Ready-made itineraries

Routes are ordered (each "→" is the next stop). Restaurants/cafés in the list are the breakfast/lunch/snack/dinner stops.

### Couples — 1 day · White Town & Serenity Beach
**Route:** Cafe des Arts → Sri Aurobindo Ashram → Our Lady of Angels Church → Bharathi Park → Maison Perumal → Serenity Beach → Zuka → Old Lighthouse → Promenade Beach → Bay of Buddha
**Why:** A slow-romance White Town morning — courtyard breakfast, the Ashram's calm, the blush-pink church and a stroll through Bharathi Park. Heritage lunch at Maison Perumal, then scenic Serenity Beach. Back for a shared Zuka dessert, golden hour by the Old Lighthouse and Promenade, and a rooftop dinner at Bay of Buddha.

### Families — 1 day · Boat, beaches & market
**Route:** Sri Murugan Cafe → Chunnambar Boat House → Jallikattu Restaurant → Paradise Beach → Daddy Amma Momo Shop → Manakula Vinayagar Temple → Goubert Market → Promenade Beach → free time → Copper Kitchen
**Why:** Quick south-Indian breakfast, then drive south for the Chunnambar backwater boat to Paradise Beach. A sit-down lunch at Jallikattu (opposite the boat house) fuels the crossing; kids snack on momos at Daddy Amma after the beach. Back in town for the evening: Manakula temple (reopens 4 PM), Goubert Market, a Promenade sunset, then dinner at Copper Kitchen.

### Friends / Bachelors — 1 day · Surf beaches & nightlife
**Route:** Baker Street → Kallialay Surf School → Serenity Beach → Terrassen Cafe → Auroville Beach → Casablanca → Promenade Beach → Catamaran Brewing Company
**Why:** Fuel up at Baker Street, then hit the north surf belt while fresh — a morning lesson at Kallialay on Serenity, beach time, lunch at Terrassen, and a second dip at Auroville Beach. Roll into White Town for shopping at Casablanca and a Promenade sunset, then close with craft beers at Catamaran Brewing Company.

### Solo — 1 day · Slow town culture

Lunch at Kasha Ki Aasha, the pink church, Aayi Mandapam's white monument, a promenade coffee at Le Café, golden-hour Promenade Beach, dinner at La Terrace.

## Practical tips & bookings

- **Matrimandir (Auroville):** free Viewing-Point pass collected in person at the Auroville Visitor Centre; the inner-chamber meditation needs an advance online pass (a few days ahead). Closed Sundays/Tuesdays.
- **Paradise Beach:** reached only by the Chunnambar backwater ferry (Chunnambar Boat House, ~8 km south); buy tickets at the boat house, last boat ~2:30 PM.
- **Scuba / surfing:** book a Discover-Scuba dive (Temple Adventures) or a surf lesson (Kallialay, Serenity Beach) a day ahead.
- **Best time to visit:** October–March is pleasant; the SW monsoon (roughly June–September) brings warm, showery afternoons — carry an umbrella.
- **Getting there:** ~3–4 hr drive from Chennai on the ECR (East Coast Road); nearest railhead is Villupuram (~38 km).

## FAQ

**How many days do you need in Pondicherry?** One to two days covers the highlights. A single day fits White Town's French Quarter, the Promenade, the Sri Aurobindo Ashram and a beach; add a second day for Auroville and Matrimandir, or the Chunnambar backwater boat to Paradise Beach.

**What is a good one-day Pondicherry itinerary?** Spend the cool morning in White Town — the Ashram before its midday close, Bharathi Park and a heritage café — then lunch, an afternoon at Serenity or Paradise Beach, a Promenade sunset and dinner.

**Best Pondicherry itinerary for couples, families or solo travellers?** Couples enjoy a slow White-Town-and-beach day ending with a rooftop dinner; families do well with the Chunnambar boat, Paradise Beach and Goubert Market; solo travellers like a culture trail of the Ashram, galleries and the heritage library.
