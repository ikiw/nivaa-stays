FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Nivaa Stays near JIPMER Pondicherry?
Yes. Nivaa Stays is located near JIPMER, Puducherry — a short drive away. It's ideal for patient families, caregivers, students, doctors, and conference visitors.
Can I book the full house?
Yes. The full-house option accommodates up to 8 guests across our 2 premium rooms + shared living spaces. Ideal for families or groups who want complete privacy.
Is Nivaa Stays pet-friendly?
Yes — we welcome pets. Please let us know when booking so we can prepare the room accordingly.
Do you serve food?
Yes. We offer home-cooked meals on request — vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and our specialty seafood dishes. Please request meals at the time of booking or on arrival.
Do you have bike rentals?
Yes. Two-wheeler rentals are available at the property — convenient for exploring Puducherry, getting to JIPMER, or running errands.
Can you arrange birthday or anniversary decorations?
Absolutely. We arrange custom celebration decorations — balloons, rose-petal setups, cake, bathtub rose-petal baths for anniversaries, and more. Tell us the occasion at booking and we'll handle the setup.
What are the room rates?
Weekday rates start at ₹2,000/night, regular weekends at ₹2,500/night, and long weekends (Fri+Sat+Sun beside a public holiday) at ₹3,000/night. Use code NIVAA10 when booking through our website for an extra 10% off.
Do the rooms have bathtub facilities?
Yes — our rooms include spacious bathtub bathrooms with premium tiling.
What are check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 12:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Flexible timing can often be arranged for full-house bookings — please ask.
Is Nivaa Stays suitable for students and parents visiting JIPMER?
Absolutely. It's popular with students joining JIPMER, families visiting for counselling or exams, and anyone needing more space than a hotel room.
Is there security and on-site assistance?
Yes — the property has 24/7 CCTV surveillance and on-call guest assistance for check-ins and any help during your stay.
How do I book?
